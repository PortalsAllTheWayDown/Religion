Matthew 2:1 - Jesus was born in Bethlehem. wise men came from the east to Jerusalem, saying where is he that is born king of the jews? for we have seen his star in the east and are come to worship him.
Herod the king at the time was troubled, gathered together the priests and scribes he demanded to know where the Christ should be born.
They informed him Bethlehem. He informed the wise men to tell him when they have found the new born king.
God warned the wise men to not return to herod the king.
The angel of the Lord appeared to Joseph in a dream and informed him to flee into Egypt because Herod is seeking to destroy the child.
Herod then killed all the children that were in Bethlehem and in all the coasts thereof from two years old and under.

Matthew 2:23 - After Joseph left Egypt with Jesus he dwelt in Nazareth that it might be fulfilled which was spoken by the prophets, He shall be called a Nazarene.

Matthew 3:1 - John the Baptist preaching in wilderness of judea. Repent ye, for the kingdom of heaven is at hand.

Matthew 3:13 - Jesus came to be baptized by John and John said, I have need to be baptized of thee and comest thou to me?
When He was baptized the heavens were opened unto him and he say the Spirit of GOd descending like a dove and lighting upon him.

Matthew 4:1 - Jesus led up of the spirit into the wilderness to be tempted of the devil. When he had fasted forty days and forty night, he was afterward an hungred.
When the tempter came to him he said if thou be the Son of God command that these stones be made bread.
But He answered nad said it is written man shall not live by bread alone but by every word that proceedeth out of the mouth of God.
Then the devil taketh him up into the holy city and setteth him on a pinnacle of the temple and saith unto him if you be the Son of God cast thyself down:
for it is written, He shall give his angels charge concerning thee: and in their hands they shall bear thee up. lest at any time thou dash thy foot against a stone.
Jesus said unto him, it is written again, Thou shalt not tempt the Lord thy God.
The devil then showed him all the kingdoms on the Earth saying if you will fall down and worship me I will give them to you.
Christ responded get thee hence satan, for it is written, thou shalt worship the Lord thy God and him only shalt thou serve.
